Acerca de Pukwudgie

Pukwudgie es una banda de grunge que mezcla sonidos del punk y metal para generar un sonido único. Una explosión de arte, rock y psicodelia que nunca podrás olvidar.

Miembros

Mali Mothman - Vocalista Principal

Mali no solo compone y canta las canciones de la banda. Además es la artista oficial de todo el universo Pukwudg

Maria Lopez - Vocals & Rhythm Guitar

Maria’s powerful vocals carry the emotional weight of our songs. Her rhythm guitar complements Alex’s leads, creating the rich textures that define our sound.

Javier Cruz - Bass

Javier holds down the groove with his deep bass lines. His steady rhythm keeps the band tight and grounded during even the wildest shows.

Sofia Morales - Drums

Sofia’s dynamic drumming drives the energy forward. Her creative beats and impeccable timing make her an essential force behind the band’s sound.
